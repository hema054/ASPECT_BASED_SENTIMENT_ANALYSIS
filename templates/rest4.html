Dive into Delicacies

Welcome to C Salt, where your culinary journey begins. Originally known as Comptons, C Salt has a storied legacy, crafted by the Usina family on a pristine piece of oceanfront property in tranquil North Beach. Since its inception in 1989, the restaurant has been a symbol of the family's commitment to providing guests with an unforgettable dining experience. Here, tradition meets modernity, and each dish tells a tale of the ocean's bounty.
